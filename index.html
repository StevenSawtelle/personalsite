About Me

Hi, I'm Steven Sawtelle! I'm a 20 year old Computer Science Junior in Barrett, the Honors College at Arizona State University. I've worked at Performance Software and ViaSat, Inc. as a Software Engineering Intern. I am currently studying abroad at the National University of Singapore, where I am taking classes in Advanced Data Structures and Algorithms, Game Design, Bollywood Studies, and Linguistics.

My dream is to work in the field of Educational Technology and bring Computer Science courses to the world. To this end, I have worked with local school districts as well as local clubs to bring programming courses into the schools of all children, and have participated in mentorship programs across Phoenix, Arizona to help students get into coding.
